A NON-CANON ARKNIGHTS RP

LINGERING ECHOES is a non-canon, sandbox Arknights RP set before the events told in the game. Oripathy, a dreaded terminal disease. It remains an issue for all Terran life, but some have decided to approach how they deal with it in different ways.

Rhodes Island maintains a somewhat peaceful approach and searches for a cure, while Reunion and its members have had enough of the treatment of Infected and have resorted to drastic measures. This conflict has continued to spread across the continent and many Terrans are quickly becoming aware of the on-going battle between the two factions.

It is only a matter of time before Terra is plunged into chaos, and it is up to you to decide which side you are on.
